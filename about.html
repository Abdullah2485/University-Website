About Us

We are the best university in the World

Welcome to Al Sheikh Abdullah bin Amer, where innovation meets tradition in a dynamic environment dedicated to academic excellence and global impact. Founded on the principles of integrity, diversity, and leadership, we are committed to providing a transformative educational experience that empowers students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our university, located in the heart of Riyadh, offers a diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs designed to inspire and challenge. With cutting-edge facilities, renowned faculty, and a vibrant campus community, we create an environment where ideas flourish and students are prepared to become leaders in their fields.

We embrace a global perspective, fostering connections with institutions and communities worldwide. Our commitment to research, innovation, and holistic education ensures that our students gain the skills and knowledge needed to address the complex challenges of the future.

At Al Sheikh Abdullah bin Amer, we believe in nurturing talent, promoting critical thinking, and encouraging lifelong learning. Join us and become part of a community dedicated to making a meaningful impact locally and globally.
